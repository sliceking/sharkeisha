My mom was convinced I was some sort of genius and was really trying to manifest a world saving, card carrying mensa member. There were so many weird expectations I had in early relationships because of what I thought a relationship was, by watching my parents. Everyone grows up thinking the stuff that goes on in their house is normal. There are probably some households where the kids grow up in terrible conditions and think "this is fucked up and not right", and I think there is a phase all kids get where they think nothing their parents do is right.
